Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our legal services

Common Questions

How do I find the right lawyer for my case?

Use our search tool to filter lawyers by practice area, location, experience, and rating. You can also read reviews and view profiles to make an informed decision. Our matching system helps connect you with legal professionals who specialize in your specific legal needs.

What are the fees for using Legal Aid?

Legal Aid is free to use for finding and connecting with lawyers. However, individual lawyers set their own consultation and service fees. Many offer free initial consultations, which will be clearly indicated on their profiles.

How do I schedule a consultation with a lawyer?

Simply click the "Contact" button on any lawyer's profile card. Fill out the contact form with your details and legal issue description. The lawyer will respond directly to schedule a consultation at their earliest convenience.

Are all lawyers on Legal Aid verified?

Yes, all lawyers on our platform are licensed professionals. We verify their credentials, bar membership, and standing before they can create a profile. We also regularly monitor reviews and feedback to maintain quality standards.

What if I'm not satisfied with a lawyer's service?

While Legal Aid facilitates the initial connection, the professional relationship is directly between you and the lawyer. However, you can leave feedback and reviews on our platform to help other users. For serious concerns, contact our support team.

Can I get legal advice directly through Legal Aid?

Legal Aid is a directory service that connects you with qualified lawyers. We don't provide direct legal advice. For specific legal guidance, you'll need to consult with one of the licensed attorneys in our network.
